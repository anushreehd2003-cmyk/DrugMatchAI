How DrugMatch AI Works

  1. Upload Prescriptions: Click on an upload card to add photos or PDF files from different doctors.
  2. AI Extraction: Our system uses advanced AI to securely extract all medication names, dosages, and instructions.
  3. Compare Medications: Once at least two prescriptions are added, click the **"Compare Prescriptions"** button.
  4. Interaction Analysis: The AI cross-references all medications to identify dangerous drug-drug interactions.
  5. Receive Alerts: View instant, color-coded alerts and safety recommendations right on the results screen.
